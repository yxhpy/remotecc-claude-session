# Code of Conduct

This repository expects respectful, technical, and direct collaboration.

## Our Standards

- Be respectful and specific.
- Focus on facts, reproduction, and proposed fixes.
- Avoid harassment, hostility, and personal attacks.
- Assume good intent, but raise risks clearly when they matter.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to issues, pull requests, discussions, and review interactions in this repository.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove content or restrict participation for conduct that makes collaboration unsafe or unproductive.
