# Xipu Campus Prototype Brief

## Goal

Build a high-fidelity homepage prototype for Southwest Jiaotong University Xipu Campus under `demo/xipu-campus/`.

The output should feel like a serious campus portal rather than a generic education landing page.

## Implementation Constraints

- Only write files under `demo/xipu-campus/`
- Prefer a static prototype with `index.html`, `styles.css`, and optional `app.js`
- No build step
- Responsive on desktop and mobile
- Use Chinese UI copy
- Avoid external UI frameworks

## Visual Direction

- Tone: calm, institutional, modern, transport-engineering identity
- Palette: deep blue, rail-silver, warm white, muted gold accents
- Avoid flashy startup aesthetics
- Use strong page hierarchy, large hero, structured information blocks, campus-map-style framing, and meaningful motion only where necessary

## Content Guidance

The page is a prototype, not an official reproduction, but it should stay grounded in official campus facts.

## Verified Official Facts

1. Xipu campus address: Sichuan, Chengdu, Pidu District, Xi'an Road 999, postcode 611756.
   Source: https://www.swjtu.edu.cn/

2. Xipu is described as the university's main campus, with administrative offices and most colleges and laboratories located there.
   Source: https://sie.swjtu.edu.cn/xysh/jtxx.htm

3. The official site emphasizes sections such as school overview, talent cultivation, scientific research, campus life, admissions and employment, news, notices, and data.
   Source: https://www.swjtu.edu.cn/

4. Xipu campus teaching building 3 won the Luban Prize and is described as a major landmark building near the east gate and the library.
   Source: https://leeds.swjtu.edu.cn/info/1032/1061.htm

5. Recent official campus updates highlight quality-of-life and infrastructure improvements, including parking, public services, and "幸福、和谐、美丽" campus construction.
   Source: https://news.swjtu.edu.cn/info/1011/64575.htm
